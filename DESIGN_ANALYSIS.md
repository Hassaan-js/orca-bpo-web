5. **Stats Section**: Large numbers with icons and descriptions
6. **Carousel/Slider**: Multiple sections with carousel functionality
7. **Campaign Grid**: Multi-column layout with campaign cards
8. **Footer**: Multi-column footer with links and contact info

## Design Direction for Orca

### Chosen Aesthetic: Premium Dark Tech with Blue Accents
- **Movement**: Modern B2B SaaS with cosmic/tech undertones
- **Core Principles**:
  1. Professional yet approachable - convey expertise without coldness
  2. Dynamic and forward-thinking - show innovation and growth
  3. Trustworthy and transparent - emphasize reliability and client success
  4. Scalable and modern - reflect ability to handle enterprise needs

- **Color Philosophy**:
  - Deep navy/charcoal background (#0f172a or #1a1f3a) - professional, stable
  - Bright blue accents (#3b82f6 or #2563eb) - energy, trust, tech
  - White/light text - clarity and readability
  - Subtle gradients - depth without distraction

- **Typography System**:
  - Display: Bold sans-serif (e.g., Space Grotesk, Poppins Bold) for headlines
  - Body: Clean sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Poppins) for content
  - Accent: Italic or semi-bold for emphasis

- **Layout Paradigm**:
  - Full-width hero sections with asymmetric content placement
  - Generous whitespace and breathing room
  - Alternating left/right content blocks
  - Card-based component system

- **Signature Elements**:
  1. Blue glowing badges/orbs for visual interest
  2. Diagonal lines and underlines for text emphasis
  3. Large, bold typography with generous line height
  4. Subtle gradient overlays on images

- **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - Smooth hover effects on interactive elements
  - Glowing effects on buttons and badges
  - Subtle animations on scroll
  - Clear focus states for accessibility

- **Animation Guidelines**:
  - Entrance animations: Fade-in with slight scale-up
  - Hover effects: Glow, slight lift, color shift
  - Scroll animations: Parallax on hero, fade-in on sections
  - Transitions: 300-500ms for smooth feel
